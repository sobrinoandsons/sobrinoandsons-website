Bienvenidos a Sobrino and Son's

Sobrino & Son's LLC provides complete and reliable 24/7 mobile mechanical services throughout Tampa and surrounding areas. We specialize in computerized diagnostics using advanced scanning tools to accurately identify vehicle issues, along with general mechanical repairs for most makes and models. Our services include emergency roadside assistance, oil and filter changes, brake replacement (pads and rotors), battery testing, replacement, and jump starts, alternators and starters, and suspension and shock absorber repairs. We also handle belt and hose replacements, tune-ups and preventive maintenance, cooling system repairs, light inspection and repair, fault code scanning and clearing, and air conditioning diagnostics and repairs. We are committed to honest service, clear communication, and convenient on-site repairs at your home, workplace, or roadside--saving you time and eliminating the need for towing.
